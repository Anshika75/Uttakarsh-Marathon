PLAYING SPORTS LIKE THERE IS NO TOMORROW

The Utkarsh Marathon is not just a race; it's a reflection of the collective spirit and the heartbeat of the city Varanasi. It beckons you to immerse yourself in an experience that captures the very essence of the city. Covering a grueling 05 KM, 10 KM & Half Marathon, it's an exhilarating journey that transcends words – an indescribable movement that etches itself into your memory forever.

Guest of Honor: Mary Kom

We are incredibly honoured to announce that Ms. Mary Kom, the legendary Indian Boxer and Olympic Medalist, and former Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha will be the Guest of Honour at the ‘Utkarsh Marathon – 2023’.
Ms. Mary Kom is an inspiration to millions around the world. She has overcome numerous challenges to achieve her dreams, and she is a shining example of what can be accomplished through hard work, dedication, and perseverance.
We are excited to have Ms. Mary Kom join us for the ‘Utkarsh Marathon’. She is an embodiment of the marathon's spirit of determination, resilience and achievement. We know that her presence will inspire all of our participants to push themselves to their limits and achieve their earmarked goals.
On the day of the Marathon, Ms. Mary Kom will flag off the race and distribute Medals to the Winners. We look forward to welcome Ms. Mary Kom to Varanasi and to celebrating the ‘Utkarsh Marathon’ with her.

Carnival & BIB Number Distribution

Carnival & BIB Number Distribution will take place on October 28, 2023 from 10 AM till 05 PM, at Sampurnanand Sanskrit University, Varanasi, i.e. on the day before the Marathon. It's a great opportunity to pick your BIB number, meet other participants, and learn more about the race. The carnival will feature a variety of activities, including:

Green Initiative

Embrace the Green Revolution in Kashi!

In our quest for a cleaner and greener Kashi, we are committed to taking meaningful steps to protect our environment and contribute positively to society. Our Green initiative embodies our dedication to sustainability, and we're excited to share our green journey with you. As part of this initiative, we will donate a plant for every participant. We believe that small changes can make a big difference, and by planting, we are taking a giant leap towards a cleaner, greener and more sustainable future.
